Sky Garden

Location / Address

51.510881° N, -0.083751° W

Description

Sky Garden is a great place to visit, with really good views of London. You can enjoy the 360 degree view of the city - for free! They are public gardens and definitely worth a visit when you have the time. Remember to keep socially distanced!
